Characters

  • Uncle Tom
  • A very righteous and selfless man
  • Protagonist of Uncle Tom's cabin
  • Spiritually driven, and uses his faith to help him through hardship
  • A slave throughout the story, sold a few times
  • Suffers at the hands of Simon Legree a cruel slave owner
  • Aunt Chloe
  • Tom's wife
  • Plays of her complex feelings by presenting herself as a fun loving simpleton
  • Left behind as a slave with the Shelby family when her husband Tom is sold
  • Her role on the farm is the Shelby's cook
  • Arthur Shelby
  • Kentucky farm owner suffering a large debt
  • He plans to sell two of his slaves, Tom, and Eliza's young son to Mr. Haley
  • A kind hearted man, who though has good intentions, puts up with slavery, and ultimately owns a few himself
  • Stowe uses Shelby to promote the idea that slavery makes villains of all its perpetrators not just the most vile of them
  • Emily Shelby
  • Wife of Arthur
  • Kind hearted women, Christian who objects to slavery, and develops family like relationships with slaves
  • Promises Eliza that she will not sell Eliza's son
  • Helps the slaves by using her power and influence over her husband
  • George Shelby
  • Arthur and Emily's son
  • Has a close connection with the slaves, particularly Tom
  • Vows to save Tom from the cruelty he encounters due to George's fathers sale of Tom
  • After Tom's death frees all the Shelby's slaves
  • Also referred to as Mas'r George
  • George Harris
  • Married to Eliza
  • He is very intellectual, and has a deep rooted love for his family
  • Does everything he can to lead them to a proper life
  • His efforts in the escape by preventing capture and shooting Loker ultimately lead to there freedom
  • Eliza Harris
  • Wife of George
  • Flees the Shelby's farm after learning of plans for her son to be sold
  • Described as very intelligent and attractive
  • With the help of her husband, her own will power and a Quaker settlement manages to gain freedom to Canada
  • Long lost daughter of Cassy
  • Harry Harris
  • Son of Eliza and George
  • Augustine St. Clare
  • Tom's Master in New Orleans
  • Eva's father
  • Atheist, and though objects to slavery does nothing to prevent it
  • Spends most of his nights drinking
  • Treats Tom well, yet still as a slave
  • Promises Tom freedom but is killed shortly before granting it
  • Eva
  • Daughter of Augustine and Marie
  • Very religious, and represents a seemingly perfect person
  • Has strong Christian ethics and is loved by all
  • Despises slavery and believes all are created equal
  • Develops a strong bond with Tom, and is a central figure in shaping his life and attitude
  • Ms. Ophelia
  • She is the cousin of Augustine St. Clare
  • Ophelia is very bigoted and prejudiced to slaves however she is not all in favor of slavery
  • Following Eva's death and with the help of a friendship with Topsy, she sees that in fact slaves are human like everyone else
  • Marie
  • Augustine's wife
  • She is a selfish women, who cares only for herself, often whining and complaining
  • She is the opposite of many of the women that Stowe writes about in her novel
  • The Quakers
  • A Christian group that developed in England during the 17th century
  • Primarily focused on better understanding God rather then all the pageantry of other groups of Christianity.
  • Throughout the novel they help Eliza and George escape to Canada as well as many other slaves
  • Senator and Mrs. Bird
  • Mrs. Bird is a very virtuous women much like many other women represented in the book
  • She uses her position as Senator Bird's wife to provide influence with her beliefs
  • Senator Bird, though compassionate and understanding of the abolitionists, is unwilling to do his part to stop the injustice of slavery
  • Tom Loker
  • Slave Hunter employed by Mr. Haley to bring back Eliza and her son
  • Organizes a group of men to track down Eliza
  • During pursuit is shot by George Harris, however he is rescued at the will of Eliza and saved by the Quakers
  • Being saved changes his attitude and he in turn helps Eliza, George, and there son reach safety
  • Mr. Haley
  • A vile man who is a slave trader
  • Buys Tom and Eliza's son off of the Shelby's
  • Though he comes off to many as a kind man, really is quite terrible to slaves
  • Topsy
  • Young slave girl, who has wild way's and is uneducated
  • Ms. Ophelia works to educate and change the young girl
  • With Topsy's help Ms. Ophelia is changed as well, and better understands slaves
  • Follows the actions of Eva and is better for it
  • Simon Legree
  • Toms third master, he is a terrible man who overworks, and abuses his slaves
  • After two slaves escape, both of which had been used sexually by Legree, he has Tom beaten to the point of death
  • Cassy
  • A female slave, used by Legree for sex
  • It is discovered she is actually Eliza's mother, but were separated when Eliza was very young
  • Smart and quick witted, develops a plan for her and Emmeline's escape
  • Emmaline
  • Another female slave used for sexual purposes by Legree
  • She is very young and described as being very beautiful
  • Also a Devout Christian
  • Escapes with Cassy and ultimately achieves freedom
  • Ms. Ophelia works to educate and change the young girl
  • With Topsy's help Ms. Ophelia is changed as well, and better understands slaves
  • Follows the actions of Eva and is better for it
